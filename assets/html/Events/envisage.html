Envisage

“ENVISAGE” a two-day start-up Expo was planned and successfully executed on 10th & 12th December 2021 by Entrepreneurship Development Cell, BV(DU)COEP. Event primarily focused on digging in the concept of entrepreneurship in young minds and giving them exposure for the future. The magnanimous event was led by our two meritorious alumni and successful entrepreneurs on 10th, Day 1 of the event

1. Shubham Rastogi - BVP (Batch -2012)

    CEO RECRO.IO

    FOUNDER DEALOCX

2.Amod Inamdar- BVP(Batch-1998)

    Founding Partner

    Zoom Your Traffic

Day 2 of the event was led by the most awaited Veteran speaker 3. Umesh Rathod Founder Lean Campus Startups Author of famous books “The Fun of Being In A Start-Up” and “Start-Up Chanakya” Speaker’s session illuminated young minds by giving them the reality check of bringing up a start-up. Speakers shared their past experiences and adversities faced during their journey of building an enterprise and also provided students with the tips and suggestions to resolve issues and deal with situational problems in their future.

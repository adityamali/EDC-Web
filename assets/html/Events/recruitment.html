Recruitment 2021

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be) University, College of Engineering, Pune (Abbreviated as EDC BV(DU) COEP) conducted its annual recruitment recently. There was a challenging competition to get into EDC since it is well renowned among students. This year, the bar was set higher than the previous year in which we received nearly 250 applications, out of which only 100 proceeded for the Personal Interview round. Those students were selected based on their written submissions, which were assessed thoroughly by the recruiting panel. The submissions consisted of answers to the elaborative situation-based questions concerning all the domains, which tested the student's descriptive and analytical mind. The Personal Interview round conducted following the submissions on 26th December 2021 brought the spark among the students through several critical thinking and condition-based questions. Finally, a total of 44 students were chosen as the 'Junior Associates' of EDC.

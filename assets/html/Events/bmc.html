Business Model Canvas

Our upcoming event will be a return to the basics. A Business Model Canvas (BMC) is a strategic Management template used for developing new business models and documenting existing Ones. Creating a Business Model Canvas is a must-know for any budding entrepreneur. “Business Model Canvas” is EDC’s upcoming event and combines both learning and its application. Participants will have a lot to learn from Mr. Taranjeet Singh’s keynote session on The Business Model Canvas. A co-founder at Innvocon Learning, Mr. Singh has a noted Reputation for helping, educating, and inspiring entrepreneurs around the world. The event will Include a quiz on the Business Model Canvas and a Creation Phase wherein participants would Be asked to create a Business Model Canvas on start-ups launched by our very own BV(DU)COEP Alumni. This event plans to test the creative and organizational skills of the participant’s while introducing them to the nitty-gritty minutiae of building start-ups and their business models.

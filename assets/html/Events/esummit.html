E Summit

Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) of Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be University College of Engineering, Pune (BV(DU)COEP) hosted its flagship event- "E-SUMMIT 2020", which served as a magnificent platform for developing entrepreneurial proficiencies in the young minds of today. The E-SUMMIT was a three-day-long event with discrete sub-events organized to develop the assorted aspects of entrepreneurial distinctiveness in students. The 30-hour long "HACKATHON" required students belonging to various technical and non-technical environments to perform together and work out a dilemma. In the end, the respective collusions came up with practical solutions for the same, thus devoting themselves to solving the problems of an uncertain world. We also had various illuminating workshops on affairs pertinent to the technological and financial aspects of the life of an entrepreneur. To conclude with day three, we had our show-stopper- Start-Up Internship Placement Program (S.I.P.P). This was a heightened career fair wherein various phenomenal start-ups belonging to sundry corporate environments visited our campus to recruit the bright minds of our college as interns.

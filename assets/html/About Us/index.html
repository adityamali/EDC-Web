About Us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be) University, abbreviated as EDC BV(DU)COEP was established in September 27, 2013, by our respected Principal Hon. Dr Anand Bhalerao Sir with the vision to bridge the gap between Industry and Academia while incubating entrepreneurial culture within the Institution. EDC incorporates eight domains: Corporate Relations, Technical & Innovations, Public Relations, Marketing, Graphic Designing, Media and Outreach, Human Resources and Research & Publications.

EDC has organized numerous entrepreneurial initiatives such as Bootcamp, E-Summit, Hackitas that have helped imbibe students with vital business qualities like leadership, time management, financial planning, negotiation and research while providing a corporate environment to aid the budding minds in proving their mettle and work on their ideas tactfully. EDC creates an entrepreneurial ecosystem that provides empowering mindsets to help individuals be successful in a rapidly changing world and strives to develop keen entrepreneurial skills to enable students to take charge of their learning with freedom and help them identify opportunities, efface problems and sell their ideas.

TECHNICAL DOMAIN :-

The technical domain's tech wizards serve as the EDC's backbone. They are in charge of creating, updating, and enhancing our official website. Change is the only constant in this fast-paced world. This transformation is being driven by the technology team, which is contemplating methods to automate our work. They assist with technological issues during events and participate in hackathons to put their talents to the test. But, most importantly, because technical abilities are required in practically every profession, the technical team seeks to improve all of our technical knowledge.

The goal of the technical domain is to automate and streamline the operation of all EDC domains. Technical domains are concerned with the creation of software and websites, as well as the recommendation and guidance of data gathering, criteria, and analysis procedures.They also aid in the development of final recommendations, write and review technical project reports, and support and provide insight into community and agency issues and regulations.

CORPORATE RELATIONS :-

Every member of our Corporate Relations department is a natural communicator and networker. The Corporate Relations team must communicate with organisations and their representatives in order to invite them to our events as judges and speakers. All members are skilled in the art of persuasion, and each performance has a direct impact on the EDC's image.

All of our events are successful, in part, because of the speakers and mentors that the Corporate Relations team brings to the table. They serve as a link between our Cell and the startup world. They handle the press release, set up interviews with key industry figures, and collaborate with different disciplines to offer information and develop EDC initiatives.

PUBLIC RELATIONS :-

The Public Relations team communicates with internal teams and keeps open lines of contact with top management, edits and updates promotional material and publications, writes and distributes press releases, and arranges public relations events. The Public Relations team excels in increasing the EDC's event outreach by collaborating with societies and informing the public about our events.

When it comes to exposure, the PR staff represents EDC through networking with college friends, partnering with E-cells, and participating in startup enthusiast networks throughout the year. They also monitor message alerts, run Dare-2-Dare challenges, and do digital marketing.

RESEARCH AND PUBLICATIONS :-

Though their work is most visible, the Research and Publications team works behind the scenes. The Publications Team has a knack for putting words together. It makes use of this talent to get ideas down on paper and then put them into action. The Publications team's objectives include writing blogs, articles, and research papers. It also aids in internal communication and the creation of eye-catching social media content.

One of the EDC's most prominent faces is content and publication. The publishing team toils away at performing in-depth research on industry-related issues, coming up with new content ideas, reviewing articles before publication, and updating blogs and social media content.The capacity to correctly draught thoughts on paper necessitates a strong mastery of the language, which is a requirement of the publication team.

MEDIA AND OUTREACH :-

The Media Team's major responsibility is to strategically expand EDC's reach by developing content, commercials, engaging with audiences on social media platforms, raising knowledge about EDC through effective planning, and promoting the concept of EDC on various platforms. The Media team's soft and hard abilities constitute its foundation. In the media team, a willingness to learn is critical.

The Media team assists the EDC strategically by developing content, commercials, engaging with audiences on social media platforms, promoting the EDC on various social media platforms, and building awareness about the EDC through good preparation. The media team also hosts social media quizzes and collaborates on events with other e-cells.They cover all of our events in detail in order to better promote them around the world.

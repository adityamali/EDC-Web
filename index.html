Who Are We?

Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be) University, abbreviated as EDC BV(DU)COEP was established in September 27, 2013, by our respected Principal Hon. Dr Anand Bhalerao Sir with the vision to bridge the gap between Industry and Academia while incubating entrepreneurial culture within the Institution.

EDC incorporates eight domains: Corporate Relations, Technical & Innovations, Public Relations, Marketing, Graphic Designing, Media and Outreach, Human Resources and Research & Publications. EDC has organized numerous entrepreneurial initiatives such as Bootcamp, E-Summit, Hackitas that have helped imbibe students with vital business qualities like leadership, time management, financial planning, negotiation and research while providing a corporate environment to aid the budding minds in proving their mettle and work on their ideas tactfully.

EDC creates an entrepreneurial ecosystem that provides empowering mindsets to help individuals be successful in a rapidly changing world and strives to develop keen entrepreneurial skills to enable students to take charge of their learning with freedom and help them identify opportunities, efface problems and sell their ideas.
